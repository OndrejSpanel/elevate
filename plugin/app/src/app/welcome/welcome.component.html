A new app for a new start!
After 7 months of hard work and more than 50 liters of coffee, the brand new StravistiX App is here!
But... Why everything has been rebuild from scratch?
Because...
  • The old application was made with 2010's web technologies which tends to be deprecated in the next months: AngularJS (v1)
  • It makes more sense to produce the newest features with up to date technologies. And thus avoid an even bigger technical migration work in the upcoming years.
Geek note: Technology now being used is Angular (v5)
This new app improves existing features on some points. She's also a way more reliable than the previous one and the global performance has been increased.
So what's the roadmap from now?
Basically, every big features which have been postponed by this required technical switch:
  • Custom year progression targets: Define your targets for various set of sports and a data type (distance, time, ...)
  • Dashboard: Interrogate your history on a period. By sports, by bike, by shoes... Customisable.
  • Grid: All your activities and stats in a table. Exportable to a spreadsheet. Mostly done in old app. Rebuild required.
  • Fitness trend planner: Simulate futures efforts (stress scores) and prepare yourself for the D-Day.
  • Fitness trend for all: A simple smartphone will be sufficient to access fitness trend feature. Extension will estimate your running and cycling stress scores without heart rate monitor and/or power meters.
